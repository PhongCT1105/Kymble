# Pitch, Submission, and Judge Q&A

## Project name

**Kymble**

## Tagline

> **Kymble finds who needs you next—and shows the receipts.**

## One-line description

Kymble is an AI GTM organization built for Nimble and Kylon that learns from real customer wins, finds companies showing the same buying conditions, and turns verified evidence into human-approved pipeline actions.

## Submission description

Most AI sales products begin with a lead list and generate outreach. Kymble begins with real customer proof. Its AI coworkers analyze Nimble customer stories, turn the problems behind those wins into observable public signals, and use Nimble to search and extract live evidence from product pages, documentation, engineering posts, jobs, and launches.

Kymble then calculates transparent Nimble and Kylon fit scores, writes the accounts and evidence into an operational CRM, and coordinates the work through Kylon. Research, verification, strategy, and handoffs can run autonomously, while sensitive external actions stop for human approval.

The sponsor-focused demo helps Nimble and Kylon find their own next customers: AI companies that need reliable live-web intelligence, organized human-agent collaboration, or both.

## Judge Q&A

### Why is this not another AI SDR?

An AI SDR usually receives a list and generates outreach. Kymble creates and justifies the list by learning from customer proof, finding current evidence, rejecting weak claims, and calculating deterministic scores before recommending any action.

### Why do you need multiple AI coworkers?

The responsibilities are genuinely different: case analysis, signal design, web research, evidence verification, strategy, coordination, and human approval. Narrow roles make the work inspectable and create meaningful handoffs inside Kylon.

### What does Nimble uniquely provide?

Live web discovery and page-level extraction. Without Nimble, the system would rely on stale data, shallow snippets, or manually prepared examples.

### What does Kylon uniquely provide?

The organizational layer: shared context, coworkers, structured work, ownership, handoffs, and the place where a human approves the final action.

### Is the prospect information real?

The public customer stories and live web evidence are real and cited. Private CRM events, contacts, meetings, replies, and opportunity amounts are synthetic and visibly labeled.

### How do you prevent hallucinations?

Every public claim needs a source URL. An Evidence Verifier separately accepts or rejects claims. Scores are calculated in TypeScript. Unsupported accounts move to `Research more` or `Rejected`.

### Why start with Nimble’s case studies?

They provide real closed-won patterns and make the project directly useful to the sponsor. Alta, Qodo, and Grips demonstrate three different reasons a company needs high-quality web intelligence.

### What if Kylon has no public API?

The challenge workspace remains the primary organization surface. Kymble supports native integration when confirmed, a structured workspace handoff when not, and a clearly labeled local mirror only as an emergency fallback.

### What is the business model after the hackathon?

A B2B company connects its customer stories and CRM. Kymble continuously monitors the live web for similar buying situations, produces verified account briefs, and operates the approval workflow. Pricing can combine a platform subscription with usage-based research volume.
